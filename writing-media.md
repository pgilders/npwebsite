I write and comment on technology, politics, culture, India, and the Global South for policy, academic, and public audiences.

## Current Book

I am currently writing a non-fiction book about modern India through the lens of technology and stories, based in part on my doctoral research at the Oxford Internet Institute. I am represented by [Matthew Marland](https://rcwlitagency.com/agents/matthew-marland) at [RCW Literary Agency](https://rcwlitagency.com/clients/prakash) in London.
